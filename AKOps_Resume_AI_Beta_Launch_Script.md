### 1:00–2:00 — What it does / the problem

*[Screen: switch to live site]*

"Here's the problem — most people send out resumes without knowing if they'll actually pass an ATS system, or how well they match a specific job. AKOps Resume AI fixes that. You upload your resume, and it gives you:

- An instant score — ATS compatibility, skills match, content quality, impact and clarity
- A JD Match tool — paste a job description, and it tells you your match percentage plus exactly which skills are missing
- A Resume Builder — parses your resume into editable sections and exports a clean, polished version

It runs on local AI, it's completely free right now, and it never stores your resume — nothing gets saved on a server."
